Resolution 1326 (2000)
Adopted by the Security Council at its 4215th meeting, on 31 October 2000
The Security Council, Having examined the application of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia for admission to the United Nations (S/2000/1043), Recommends to the General Assembly that the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia be admitted to membership in the United Nations.
