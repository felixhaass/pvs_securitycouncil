RESOLUTION 1140 (1997) Adopted by the Security Council at its 3836th meeting,
on 28 November 1997
The Security Council, Recalling its resolution 1110 (1997) of 28 May 1997, 1. Decides to extend the mandate of the United Nations Preventive Deployment Force for an additional period terminating on 4 December 1997; 2. Decides to remain actively seized of the matter.
