RESOLUTION 1028 (1995) Adopted by the Security Council at its 3604th meeting,
on 8 December 1995
The Security Council, Recalling all its previous resolutions on the situation in Rwanda, in particular its resolution 997 (1995) of 9 June 1995, Having considered the report of the Secretary-General on the United Nations Assistance Mission for Rwanda (UNAMIR) of 1 December 1995 (S/1995/1002), 1. Decides to extend the mandate of UNAMIR for a period expiring on 12 December 1995; 2. Decides to remain actively seized of the matter.
