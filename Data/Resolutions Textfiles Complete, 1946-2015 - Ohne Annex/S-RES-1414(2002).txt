Resolution 1414 (2002)
Adopted by the Security Council at its 4542nd meeting, on 23 May 2002
The Security Council, Having examined the application of the Democratic Republic of East Timor for admission to the United Nations (S/2002/558), Recommends to the General Assembly that the Democratic Republic of East Timor be admitted to membership in the United Nations.
