RESOLUTION 1253 (1999) Adopted by the Security Council at its 4026th meeting,

The Security Council, Having examined the application of the Kingdom of Tonga for admission to the United Nations (S/1999/793), Recommends to the General Assembly that the Kingdom of Tonga be admitted to membership in the United Nations.
