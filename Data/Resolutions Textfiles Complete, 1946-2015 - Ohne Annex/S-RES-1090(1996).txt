RESOLUTION 1090 (1996)
Adopted by the Security Council at its 3725th (closed) meeting, on 13 December 1996
The Security Council, Having considered the question of the recommendation for the appointment of the Secretary-General of the United Nations,
Recommends to the General Assembly that Mr. Kofi Annan be appointed Secretary-General of the United Nations for a term of office from 1 January 1997 to 31 December 2001.
