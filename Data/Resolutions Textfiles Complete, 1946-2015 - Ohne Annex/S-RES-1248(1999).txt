RESOLUTION 1248 (1999) Adopted by the Security Council at its 4016th meeting
on 25 June 1999
The Security Council, Having examined the application of the Republic of Kiribati for admission to the United Nations (S/1999/477), Recommends to the General Assembly that the Republic of Kiribati be admitted to membership in the United Nations.
