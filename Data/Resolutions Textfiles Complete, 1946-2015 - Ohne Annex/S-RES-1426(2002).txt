Resolution 1426 (2002)
Adopted by the Security Council at its 4585th meeting, on 24 July 2002
The Security Council, Having examined the application of the Swiss Confederation for admission to the United Nations (S/2002/801), Recommends to the General Assembly that the Swiss Confederation be admitted to membership in the United Nations.
